Islands Fries

Ingredients

  • Kennebec or Russet potatoes
  • 50/50 combination of Peanut and Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes in to about 1/2" fries
  2. Blanch in 325 degree oil for about 3-4 minutes
  3. Pat fries dry and place in a bowl, then move to fridge to cool down for at least 30 minutes
  4. Fry again at 350 degree for 6 minutes
